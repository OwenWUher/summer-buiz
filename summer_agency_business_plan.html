How you find clients

Ryan's outreach playbook

Walk in to local gyms, barbershops, restaurants — show the portfolio on your phone

DM Instagram accounts with bad or no websites ("we build affordable sites for gyms like yours")

Google "[city] barbershop no website" — call or email directly

Post in local Facebook groups for small business owners

Offer a free audit ("your site is losing you customers — here's why")

Target signals (who to pitch)

No website or using a Link-in-bio as their "site"

Outdated site (still says 2019, broken mobile layout)

Good reviews on Google/Yelp but nothing to send people to

Active Instagram but no booking or contact info

New businesses — just opened, getting set up
